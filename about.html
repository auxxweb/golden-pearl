About us

Established in the year 2016, Golden Pearl have been serving the needs of retail, commercial and Residential construction sectors. Our ethos is excellence in design and delivery; working across architecture, master planning, interior design, and graphic design.

Our mission is to understand our clients’ cultural, functional, operational and commercial needs, drawing on our experience to facilitate solutions that work.  We strive to create the best solutions for client’s projects and to ensure high quality and timely project execution. We believe that quality and timing project implementation are very critical to the success of our business.

Golden Pearl is well placed to offer speedy execution in respect of Civil and Interior decoration with a large team of workforce of Civil, Interior and MEP Workers. The company has also created and strengthened in house capabilities for supervision and execution of large turnkey jobs involving the entire scope of interior works like Partition, Paneling, False Ceiling, Furniture and all Interior related Civil works, MEP and finishing works.

We are dedicated to give our best to satisfy every customer by having a solid network with suppliers and field workers. In the long-term business plan. The professionalism at all levels ensures that the planning and preparation gives the customer top quality results. We will always put in front that customer satisfaction is the most important thing to maintain.

All clients are provided with a full-time dedicated team that ensures adherence to budget and timelines.

Our aim is to develop design and implement the most innovative yet cost effective solution for each project irrespective of its size - whether it is fresh design or a total redesign - to increase the amount of usable space. We incorporate end-to-end process within a project by providing all the required services to design and build your interior space by managing the project from the first point of contact through to handling over the keys.
